To the Reading Radiologist
When present, comment on areas of increased signal or findings consistent with soft tissue or ligamentous injury. Pay particular attention to the upper cervical ligaments and interspinous ligaments. Assess the posterior elements of the spinal joints for derangement of the pedicles, pars, and uncovertebral joints. If, in your professional opinion, any findings are causally related to the mechanism of injury noted in the study indication within a reasonable degree of medical probability, document that conclusion in your report.
Thank you!
